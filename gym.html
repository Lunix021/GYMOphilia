SOME EXTRA TIPS:

  • 10-20 working sets are mandatory for muscle growth.

  • PROGRESSIVE OVERLOAD: Make sure you atleast increase your reps by 1 or increase the weight you lift by 2.5 kg per week.

  • Moderate–high volume + heavy-enough weight is best for muscle hypertrophy.

  • Make sure you get enough sleep of atleast 8 hours and if possible then (1.5*bodyweight) protein weight is good.

  • If u want to do another exercise u can do it , because one might feel different effects via same exercise .It is due to fact that everyone has different mobility and density of their bones and muscles respectively.

  • Do compounds first ,then the isolation exercises.

  • Make sure your form is good ,avoid ego lifting.

  • Dont worry about the light arms day because that is just growth signal , u dont actually need to go for failure this day but your muscles will continue to grow.
